Experience

Located in the heart of Island of Panglao, Mithi Resort and Spa is your perfect escape into a world of wonder. Explore our stunning surroundings and uncover the hidden gems that make every moment unforgettable. Whether you're an adventurer seeking an adrenaline rush or a traveler looking for pure relaxation, we have something just for you.

WHITE SAND BEACH

Surrounded by breathtaking landscapes and untouched beauty, Mithi Resort and Spa is the ultimate retreat. Relax, explore, and make it your own personal paradise.

CAMBAGAT CAVE

Thrill-seekers don't need to venture far for adventure. Located within Mithi Resort and Spa's expansive 14-hectare grounds is the Cambagat Cave, where you can dive and swim depending on the tides.

MITHI SPA

Restore your mind and body in Panglao's most popular and loved Spa. Let our specially trained therapists melt all your troubles away and discover for yourself why people keep coming back to Mithi Spa.
