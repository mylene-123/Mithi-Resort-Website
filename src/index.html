Mithi Resort & Spa

Discover Mithi Resort and Spa, a peaceful retreat in the heart of Bohol. Surrounded by clear waters, lush greenery, and white sand beaches, it's the perfect place to relax, unwind, and explore. Enjoy delicious cuisine, soothing spa treatments, and exciting adventures in a tropical paradise.

Choose from our well-appointed villas and suites, each designed for relaxation and stunning views of Bohol's natural beauty. Whether you prefer a beachfront escape or a private retreat surrounded by lush greenery, every stay promises tranquility and indulgence.
